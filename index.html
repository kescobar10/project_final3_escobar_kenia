About Salt & Butter

With the combination of art and talent, our dedicated chefs present you with the most elegant collection of European confections available today. Operating for over 25 years, we use only the finest natural ingredients selected from all over the world so we can bring that quality directly to you.

Founded on quality, expertise, and passion, we create the finest array of handmade bread, pastries, cakes, macarons, cookies, sandwiches, breads, pies, drinks, and chocolates imaginable.
